# House of Tartarus Card Design Standard

## Front

- House of Tartarus crest.
- Member or scene name.
- Motto: `DISCIPLINE ◆ DIGNITY ◆ DEVELOPMENT`
- Statement: `All are Welcome ◆ Respect is Required`
- QR code linked to the approved FetLife contact.

## Back

- Discord: `Email us on Fet for invite`
- FetLife: `https://fetlife.com/CaptainCronos`
- Approved email and website details.
- House of Tartarus watermark.
- Community statement and footer.

## Print preparation

- Use Avery 28878 dimensions and the original template geometry.
- Maintain the artwork aspect ratio.
- Center the artwork inside each card position.
- Keep important text and QR content inside a safe margin.
- Verify the final PDF at actual size before printing.
